Financial Planning Workshop 1 - Where is your money?

Open for all new students

Disclaimer: This is not a financial advice. This is financial planning workshop.

Who is this for?

- If you want to manage your money but do not know where to start.

- You want to be in control of your money but you are not sure, how much you can/should invest.

- You do not want to see your money sitting in your savings account, but are also afraid of risking too much.

- You have a lot of ideas, but you don't really know how much risk you can take.

- You are willing to learn, and have consumed a lot of resources online but still do not have a personal plan created.

- May be you are already have a financial plan but you still end up spending a lot.

What this session is not about?

- I am not telling you what to do with your money.

- I am not going to tell you how to make more money.

- I am not going to tell you Financial Jargons.

- I am not going to manage your money.

- This is not an informative session it is a workshop.

What will you get out of this workshop?

- A new way of looking at your personal finance.

- A bit of calrity on how you are going to save, spend and create different fund corpus.

- A tad bit of sanity and a tad bit of feeling of being in control of your money.

- A personal working sheet with your Financial forcast for the year.

Financial Planning Workshop 2 - How to choose an Investment tool?

Open for old students only. (Prerequisite Should have attended Workshop 1)

Disclaimer: This is not a financial advice. This is financial planning workshop.

Who is this for?

- If you know how much you want to save, but are confused with so many investment options.

- If you are not from the finacial background, but are interested to know how to assess an investment tool.

- If the only investment tools you end up choosing which are popular in your family and friends.

What this session is not about?

- This is not a Financial investment advice session

- You will not be asked or discouraged from investing your money in any tool.

- You will not be sold a particular investment tool.

- I am not going to manage your money.

- This is not an informative session it is a workshop.

What will you get out of this workshop?

- An understanding common mistakes that most of us do.

- A basic understanding of what are the different types of investment opportunities exist.

- A personal framework to judge any investment tool.

- A personal working sheet to create your own investment plan.
